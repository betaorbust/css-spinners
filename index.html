CSS Loading Spinners

The top Code Pen from 2014 was 8 animated SVG loading spinners. Here are the same spinners, rewritten in pure HTML and CSS. They're efficient and work in IE 10+. (IE<10 can be supported via a background gif fallback.)

Loading indicator with a dot running around a circle
Loading indicator made by rotating a quarter of a circle's border around continuously.
Loading indicator made by rotating a half of a circle's border around continuously.
Loading indicator with three vertical bars wiggling from a static top position.
Loading indicator with three vertical vars wiggling vertically in a continuous wave.
Loading indicator with three vertical bars expanding in turn along a central axis.
Loading indicator with three vertical bars fading in and out sequentially.
Loading indicator with three vertical bars fading in and out and expanding and contracting sequentially.
